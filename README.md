**Retail Business Analytics Dashboard**

A collaborative end-to-end analytics project built to showcase job-ready skills in Python, Power BI, marketing analytics, supply chain analytics, forecasting, and business storytelling.

**Project Goal**
Analyze an online retail business using real transactional data.
Understand customer behavior and revenue performance.
Improve inventory planning and product prioritization.
Build interactive dashboards for executive decision-making.
Present the project as a professional portfolio case study for recruiters.

**Project Workflow**
Raw retail dataset
Python data cleaning
ETL and preprocessing
Feature engineering
Exploratory data analysis
Marketing analytics
Supply chain analytics
Machine learning and forecasting
Power BI dashboard development
Business insights and recommendations

**Data Cleaning and ETL**
Imported the raw Online Retail II dataset into Python.
Identified data quality issues such as missing values, duplicates, negative quantities, cancelled invoices, invalid prices, and inconsistent date formats.
Removed duplicate transactions.
Removed missing customer IDs and missing product descriptions.
Removed cancelled and invalid transactions.
Converted invoice dates into proper datetime format.
Created a clean master dataset for analysis.
Built new business variables such as revenue, year, month, month-year, customer metrics, product metrics, and product categories.

**Marketing Analytics**
Analyzed customer behavior and revenue contribution.
Created customer KPIs such as total customers, total orders, average order value, revenue per customer, and purchase frequency.
Performed RFM analysis using recency, frequency, and monetary value.
Segmented customers into groups such as Champions, Loyal Customers, At Risk Customers, and Lost Customers.
Conducted Market Basket Analysis to identify products frequently purchased together.
Identified opportunities for cross-selling, bundling, retention campaigns, and targeted promotions.

**Supply Chain Analytics**
Analyzed product demand, quantity sold, revenue, and category performance.
Studied monthly demand trends and seasonal patterns.
Identified fast-moving and slow-moving products.
Applied linear trend analysis to understand long-term revenue movement.
Used Holt-Winters forecasting to predict future demand.
Measured forecast accuracy using MAPE, MAD, and MSD.
Performed ABC inventory classification to prioritize high-value products.
Calculated safety stock and reorder points for inventory planning.
Created inventory priority recommendations for better replenishment decisions.

**Power BI Dashboards**
Built three interactive dashboards:
Executive Dashboard
Marketing Dashboard
Supply Chain Dashboard

**Executive Dashboard**
Tracks total revenue, total orders, total customers, average order value, and total products.
Shows revenue trends, top products, country performance, category performance, and year-over-year insights.

**Marketing Dashboard**
Highlights customer segments, customer value, RFM results, market basket insights, and revenue contribution by segment.
Supports campaign targeting, retention strategy, and customer growth decisions.

**Supply Chain Dashboard**
Displays demand trends, forecasting results, ABC classification, inventory planning, safety stock, reorder points, and product priorities.
Supports inventory optimization and operational planning.
